First Order Differential Equations

Differential equations are the most important part in modeling physical systems. And they are very abstracted, and really difficult to think about how they really works. Even though we might be able to solve them without knowing what they really are, or how they are describing the world. So I made this app to help you imagine what the first order linear differential equations are by plotting their slope fields (isoclines). The solution to the slope field is the one that follow the slope line. And I will add the solutions after I finish my exam. I will explain more on that on my blog Foundational Understanding
